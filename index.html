Welcome

Hats as unique as the head that wears them

Whether you are searching for the perfect finishing touch to an outfit, a thoughtful gift, or a bold statement piece, we have you beautifully covered. Every hat in our Walcot Street boutique is chosen and finished with meticulous care, so that each one feels as individual as the person wearing it.

We specialise in fitting and personalising hats for every occasion — weddings, race days, garden parties and festivals — with an unhurried, one-to-one service you will remember long after you leave.
